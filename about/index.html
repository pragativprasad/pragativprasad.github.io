About

Hello! My name is Pragati and I am an epidemiologist. I’m currently a PhD student.

In an effort to deepen my scientific capabilities, I’ll be posting my adaptions of published infectious disease models in Posts. You may find some other modelling and epidemiology content there as well.

I have a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from the University of Texas at Austin and a Master of Public Health in Epidemiology from Emory University. I worked for 3 years at CDC as an ORISE Fellow / Health Scientist and for 2 years as a Scientific Software Developer at a start-up prior to my masters.

Experience & Interests:

  • Infectious disease models
  • Policy and guidance for diseases with epidemic or pandemic potential
  • Human mobility, contacts, and social epidemiology
  • Computational biology and bioinformatics
  • Government and non-governmental organization innovation
  • Healthcare and public health start-ups
